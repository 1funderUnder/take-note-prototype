About the Take Note Music App

At Take Note, we know the frustrations that go along with practicing music - the excitement and the joy of improvement, too. This app was built to help musicians at all levels stay organized, stay motivated, and most importantly, keep making music. Remember, every note practiced brings you one step closer to musical perfection. Progress happens when you show up daily. Your music matters, and this app is here to help you make it happen.
